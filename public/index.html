Functions and Features

Single Row Fetching

A single number fetches its corresponding row.

E.g., "7" fetches data from row 7

Specific Row Fetching

Numbers separated by ',' fetch individual corresponding rows.

E.g., "7,9,10" fetches data from row 7,9 and 10

Row Range Fetching

Numbers separated by '-' fetch a range of rows.

E.g., "7-10" fetches data from row 7 to 10
